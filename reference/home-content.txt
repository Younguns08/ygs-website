Hey parents and students!
I'm Pete, founder of Young Guns.
After two decades of teaching, here's what I know: school rewards compliance and right answers. It's a race to the middle that leaves the truly brilliant kids behind.
Roy was seen as distracted, unfocused, and always restarting. His fast, nonlinear mind made traditional school hard, if not impossible. Teachers' favorite report card comment? He wasn't reaching his potential.
But he wasn't behind, he was ahead. He just needed someone to see him.
Together, we worked on his mindset and voice, unearthing his brilliance underneath the chaos. He built a powerful portfolio of projects that mattered to him, including sensory instruments, a chair for forest bathing, a manifesto about perception, to give some examples. The result? Roy was accepted to USC's Iovine & Young Academy, one of the most selective innovation programs in the world.
Now he's found his tribe. He's building apps with AI, joining hackathons in Shanghai, working with developers and entrepreneurs at IYA.
I built YG for kids like Roy. Students who can think creatively, take risks, and build original ideas the world hasn't seen before.
Don't let your child's spark get buried in compliance.
Let's build the story only they can tell.
